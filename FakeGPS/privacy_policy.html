Collecting and Using Information

Location Information

The Application may request access to precise or approximate device location to show your current GPS position, set the initial marker position, operate mock location features selected by you, and show foreground service status while mock location or delayed movement is running.

Location access is used for Application functionality. We do not sell your location information.

Mock Location Information

When you choose a location and start mock location, the Application uses Android mock location APIs to apply the selected latitude and longitude to your device. This may affect other apps that read device location, except apps or services that block or detect mock locations.

Favorite Locations

If you save a favorite location, the Application stores the favorite name, latitude, and longitude locally on your Device. This information is used only to display saved favorites and move the marker to a selected saved location.

Search and Geocoding

When you search for an address, the Application sends the search query and, when available, the current marker coordinate to NAVER Cloud Geocoding services to return address search results. Search results may include address text and geographic coordinates.

Advertising and Device Identifiers

The Application uses Google AdMob to show ads, including banner ads, app open ads, rewarded ads, and rewarded interstitial ads. Google and its partners may collect and use information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interactions, approximate location, and diagnostic data to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads.

Depending on your region and settings, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. Where required by law, consent may be requested before personalized ads or related storage technologies are used.

The Application connects to our remote app settings endpoint to load configuration such as app version, update information, links, and ad unit configuration. The Application also checks whether an internet connection is available so online features can work correctly.

These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Favorite locations are stored locally on your Device. You may remove this data by clearing the Application's storage or uninstalling the Application. The Application does not require an account and does not provide a cloud sync feature for favorites.

You can manage permissions in your device settings. Some Application features may not work if required permissions are denied.

Mock Location Notice

To use mock location features, you must enable Android Developer Options and select this Application as the mock location app where required by your device. After using the Application, we recommend turning off mock location settings to avoid incorrect real GPS behavior in other apps.
